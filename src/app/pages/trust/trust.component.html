  • ⚙️structured team generation workflows
  • 📊balanced configuration structures

🔐 YOUR DATA, PROTECTED

Your data safety comes first. Here is exactly how we protect it.

We collect only what’s needed to run the service, never sell it, and use optional analytics or ads cookies only with consent. Here is precisely who can touch your data — and why.

💳

Card details never reach us

Payments run through Razorpay (PCI-DSS certified). Your card number and CVV go straight to Razorpay and never touch PICK2WIN’s servers — we only receive confirmation that a payment succeeded. There is nothing card-related for us to lose.

🚫

No selling, consent first

We never sell your personal data. Essential cookies keep the service running, and optional Google Analytics or Google Ads cookies load only after you accept them — details in our Cookie Policy.

🤝

Minimal sharing, single purpose

We share only what’s strictly required with the providers that run the service: Razorpay (payment), our SMS / email provider (your verification code only), and our football-data provider (lineups flow in to us). They are bound by contract and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

🗑️

Your data, your control

We keep your data only while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time and your personal data is erased (limited records may be kept only where law requires) — see our Privacy Policy.

⚙ OUR INFRASTRUCTURE PARTNERS

We rely on best-in-class providers. And we tell you who they are.

PICK2WIN does not build everything in-house. For payments and football data — the two systems users care about most — we partner with proven, regulated providers and disclose them publicly.

Razorpay
PAYMENT GATEWAY PARTNER

All payments processed by Razorpay.

Every transaction — coin-pack purchase — runs through Razorpay with 3D Secure authentication, Radar fraud detection, and card fingerprint deduplication. PICK2WIN never stores card numbers. Razorpay holds PCI compliance, the dispute resolution flow, and the chargeback process. We direct users to Razorpay Checkout and receive only the confirmation event.

⚽ DATA
FOOTBALL API PARTNER

Football data sourced from our third-party football API provider.

All starting XIs, substitute lists, player metadata, fixture timings, and live match status come from a third-party football API provider — covering exactly the leagues PICK2WIN operates across. When the provider publishes a starting XI, our UCT window opens. When the provider updates a substitute, our engine reflects it. PICK2WIN does not maintain its own lineup database.
