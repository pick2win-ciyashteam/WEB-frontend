Our infrastructure partners

We rely on best-in-class providers. And we tell you who they are.

PICK2WIN does not build everything in-house. For payments and football data, we partner with proven providers and disclose them publicly.

stripe
Payment gateway partner

All payments processed by Stripe.

Every transaction runs through Stripe with 3D Secure authentication, Radar fraud detection, and card fingerprint deduplication. PICK2WIN never stores card numbers. Stripe holds PCI compliance, dispute resolution, and chargeback flow.

SportMonks
Football API partner

Football data sourced from SportMonks.

All starting XIs, substitute lists, player metadata, fixture timings, and live match status come from SportMonks. When SportMonks publishes a starting XI, the UCT window opens. PICK2WIN does not maintain its own lineup database.

⚖️
Transparency notice - player mismatches

If our players do not match your fantasy app's playing XI...

Stick to your fantasy app's player list - not ours. If one or a few players do not appear in that app's playing XI, your fantasy app is the source you should follow. Adjust your final contest entry to match what your fantasy app shows.

This is rare. In most matches, PICK2WIN's playing XI matches your fantasy app exactly. Mismatches can happen when our football API partner SportMonks publishes incorrect or delayed lineup data for a specific match. We acknowledge this rare-but-possible scenario upfront.

PICK2WIN's primary responsibility is being transparent to all users.
