Transparency Notice · Lineup Updates After UCT Generation

When lineups change after you have already generated your UCT teams.

PICK2WIN relies on third-party football data providers for official lineup information. In rare situations, a lineup may be updated after users have already generated their UCT teams.

  • a player is removed from the Playing XI
  • another player is added to the Playing XI
  • lineup information is corrected or updated by the data provider

These situations are outside the direct control of PICK2WIN and may occur occasionally due to external data updates.

Our Approach

If such lineup updates are received after UCT generation:

  • users who have not yet generated teams will automatically see the latest available lineup information
  • users who have already generated teams may see a notification or broadcast message under My Teams
  • the update information is provided as a transparency and reference feature
User Notification

Where possible, PICK2WIN will display updated player information, affected match details, and lineup change notifications within the platform for user awareness.

Important

UCT generation completed using the lineup information available at the time of generation.

Lineup updates received later do not automatically trigger UCT regeneration, additional UCT runs, or additional coin deductions.

The purpose of the notification is simply to keep users informed when post-generation lineup updates are received from external data providers.

Our Commitment

PICK2WIN believes transparency is important.

When lineup updates are received after team generation, we will make reasonable efforts to communicate those updates within the platform so users remain informed and can make their own decisions accordingly.
