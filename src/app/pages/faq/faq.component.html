Section 3

LINEUP CONFIGURATION & TEAM GENERATION

13 How does PICK2WIN work?

PICK2WIN simplifies the process of creating multiple football lineups while ensuring that every important football decision remains under your control.

The workflow is straightforward:

Step 1 – Select Fantasy Platform

Choose your preferred platform:

  • DraftKings Classic DFS
  • FanDuel Classic DFS

Step 2 – Select Players

Choose your own player pool from the official match lineup.

Step 3 – Select Eligible Substitutes (Optional)

Optionally choose eligible substitute players to be considered during team generation.

Step 4 – Apply Mandate YES (Optional)

Mark eligible outfield players that you want included in every generated team.

Step 5 – Generate Teams

PICK2WIN processes your selections and generates up to 20 structured football teams in just a few seconds.

Step 6 – Preview & Download

Review your generated teams and download the TXT file containing your complete configuration and all generated teams.

Throughout the entire workflow, every football decision comes from you. PICK2WIN simply organizes your selections into structured lineups.

14 When can I generate teams?

Team generation becomes available only after the official starting lineups for a match have been released.

Once the official lineups are available, you can complete the lineup configuration process and generate your teams until the match officially starts.

This ensures your generated teams are based on the latest confirmed player availability.

15 Can I generate teams more than once for the same match?

No.

Each successful generation consumes one Coin and allows one successful generation per user for that match.

Once your teams have been generated:

  • The associated Coin is consumed.
  • Your teams become available in Preview Teams.
  • You can review and download your TXT file until the match starts.

This approach keeps the generation process simple and fair for all users.

16 How does substitute selection work?

Substitute selection is completely optional.

You may select up to three (3) substitute players to be included in the lineup generation process.

Important rules:

  • Only DEF, MID, and FWD substitute players can be selected.
  • Goalkeepers cannot be selected as substitutes.
  • You may also choose not to select any substitutes.

Substitute selection gives you additional flexibility while keeping every selection entirely under your control.

17 What is Mandate YES?

Mandate YES is an optional feature that allows you to ensure selected eligible outfield players appear in every generated team.

If a player is marked as Mandate YES, PICK2WIN includes that player in all generated teams.

Players that are not marked as Mandate YES remain as N/A, allowing the generation engine to naturally create balanced team combinations from your selected player pool.

Important rules:

  • Mandate YES is optional.
  • It applies only to eligible outfield players.
  • Goalkeepers cannot be assigned Mandate YES.

You remain fully responsible for deciding which players, if any, receive Mandate YES.
