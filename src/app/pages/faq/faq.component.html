07 / FAQ

Common questions.

/ 01Is my first UCT really free?
Yes. Every PICK2WIN account gets one free UCT generation after you sign up and complete dual verification (mobile SMS OTP + email OTP). No card required to claim. You choose which upcoming match window to use it on - full 20-team generation, no charge. The free claim must be used within 14 days of verification. From your second match onward, choose any coin pack - Starter (10), Basic (25), Standard (50), or Pro (100). All packs valid for 365 days from purchase, fully your choice.
/ 02Can I open multiple accounts to keep claiming free matches?
No. Each account requires both a verified mobile number (SMS OTP) and a verified email (email OTP) - neither alone is enough. One mobile = one email = one account. We also enforce email normalisation, disposable-email blocking, VoIP/virtual-number blocking, Stripe Radar device fingerprinting, IP velocity caps, and behavioural analysis. Accounts created to exploit the free UCT offer are permanently banned.
/ 03Why do you need my mobile number?
Two reasons. One: to ensure one account per real user. Two: to send a one-time SMS code so we can verify you are a real person before activating UCT. We do not call, message, or share your number for marketing.
/ 04Is PICK2WIN a gambling or betting platform?
No. PICK2WIN is a workflow assistance platform for fantasy football preparation. It is not a gambling operator, betting platform, contest host, wallet service, or fantasy contest provider. No prediction. No guaranteed outcomes. Read the full PICK2WIN Platform Pledge ->
/ 05When does UCT activate?
UCT activates strictly after official football lineups are released for a match. Before lineups, the engine is dormant - there is no guesswork on team-sheets.
/ 06Can I run UCT more than once per match?
No. Generation is one-time per match per user. After UCT creates your 20 teams, the match locks and you are redirected to My Teams. Re-generation is blocked.
/ 07Do you refund cancelled or postponed matches?
No. PICK2WIN does not refund coin pack purchases or provide match-wise compensation for cancelled, postponed, abandoned, interrupted, or weather-affected matches. Coins are issued at purchase and are non-refundable. Each pack is valid for 365 days from purchase, so unused coins remain in your account until that window closes.
/ 08Can I buy multiple coin packs and combine them?
Yes. You can purchase as many coin packs as you like and they stack into a single coin balance on your account. Since each pack is valid for 365 days from purchase, you can start small and scale up later.
/ 09Is the FIFA World Cup 2026 included in the coin packs?
Yes. Every coin pack covers all 30 PICK2WIN competitions, including the FIFA World Cup 2026. Each coin = one match UCT generation, and you can spend coins on any match in any covered league. No separate tournament passes or league-specific upgrades.
/ 10Which countries can use PICK2WIN?
PICK2WIN is available in the UK, Ireland, most of Western and Northern Europe, the United States, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. It is geo-blocked in India, China, Russia, Belarus, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nigeria, Indonesia, Turkey, Cuba, Iran, North Korea, Syria, and OFAC-sanctioned regions.
/ 11How are payments processed?
All payments are processed through Stripe with international card support, 3D Secure verification, and Stripe Radar fraud monitoring. We never hold user wallets or balances.
/ 12How long are my generated teams stored?
Generated teams are retained for the current day only and automatically cleared at midnight in your local time zone. Download or screenshot teams during the match-day window if you want to keep them.
/ 13Does UCT predict match outcomes or fantasy results?
No. UCT does not predict match outcomes, player performance, or fantasy points. It is not an AI prediction engine, not a random generator, and not a betting bot. It generates teams based on your configuration.
/ 14Who is PICK2WIN's payment partner?
Stripe is PICK2WIN's exclusive payment gateway partner. Every coin-pack transaction runs through Stripe with 3D Secure authentication, Stripe Radar fraud detection, and card fingerprint deduplication. PICK2WIN never stores card numbers.
/ 15Where does PICK2WIN's football data come from?
SportMonks is PICK2WIN's football API partner. Starting XIs, substitute lists, player metadata, fixture timings, and live match status are sourced from SportMonks. PICK2WIN does not maintain its own lineup database.
/ 16What if PICK2WIN shows a player but my fantasy app doesn't?
Always stick to your fantasy app's player list, not PICK2WIN's. If a player mismatch appears, adjust your final contest entry to match your fantasy app. This can happen rarely if football API data is delayed or incorrect. If you spot a persistent mismatch, email support@pick2win.io.
/ 17What is PICK2WIN's primary responsibility?
Transparency. We are transparent about partners, how UCT works, pricing, account rules, restricted countries, and what we do not control. If we miss something or get something wrong, we acknowledge it.
/ 18How do coin packs work? Will I be charged automatically after buying?
No auto-renewal. Ever. Every coin-pack purchase is a one-time, user-initiated payment through Stripe Checkout. There is no recurring billing, no subscription, and no background charge. You control every payment.
/ 19Is the captaincy step mandatory in UCT?
Yes. Substitute and Mandate are optional, but Captaincy is mandatory. You must pick Captain and Vice-Captain choices to continue because captaincy is structurally fundamental to the 20-team workflow.
/ 20Will PICK2WIN's teams work directly in my fantasy app's contest?
PICK2WIN is fantasy-app agnostic. It does not try to match DraftKings salary caps, FPL budgets, FanDuel formats, or another app scoring system. UCT produces structured combinations; you adapt them to whichever fantasy app you play on.
/ 21Who is PICK2WIN built for?
Multi-entry fantasy football users who create strictly 20 teams per match. If you want structured team variations with captain rotation, hedging, and exposure balancing, PICK2WIN is built for you.
/ 22What's the difference between Mandate-YES and Mandate-NO?
Mandate-YES means the player appears in every one of your 20 teams. Mandate-NO means the player appears in none of your 20 teams and cannot be selected as Captain or Vice-Captain. Both are optional.
/ 23Why doesn't PICK2WIN use coins, salary caps, or budgets like other fantasy apps?
Every fantasy platform has its own rules. PICK2WIN intentionally does not copy those systems. UCT works from official playing XI and substitutes, applying only football-real position constraints and formation splits. You adapt the output to your contest app.
/ 24Why does PICK2WIN always generate exactly 20 teams? Why not 10 or 50?
Twenty is the minimum count needed to make UCT structurally meaningful. Smaller counts cannot fulfil proper Captain rotation, Vice-Captain rotation, formation coverage, or wider exposure. Future versions may add 40-team and 60-team modes.
/ 25What's the difference between CVC mode and C & VC mode in captaincy?
CVC mode lets 2 to 4 players rotate dynamically as both Captain and Vice-Captain. C & VC mode separates Captain and Vice-Captain pools with structured count limits. Both modes assign exactly one Captain and one Vice-Captain per team.
/ 26Does PICK2WIN guarantee favourable outcomes, ranking positions, or contest results?
No. UCT does not guarantee favourable outcomes, ranking positions, or contest success. PICK2WIN is a workflow tool that supports structured team generation and reduces lineup stress. Final contest decisions remain your responsibility.
/ 27What if 3 or 4 matches release lineups at the same time?
This is where PICK2WIN saves the most time. Manually, 4 matches x about 60 minutes each creates about 240 minutes of work in a 60-minute window. With PICK2WIN, 4 matches can be configured and generated in about 20 minutes total.
/ 28Do I still need to enter the teams into my fantasy app myself?
Yes. PICK2WIN does not host contests, auto-submit entries, or integrate directly with contest apps. Once UCT generates teams, you still need to copy or use the exported team file in your fantasy contest app of choice.
/ 29What happens to my generated teams after kickoff?
Once a match goes live, the 20 teams generated for that match disappear from your My Teams view. Download the CSV before kickoff. Once downloaded, the teams are yours forever, but if you do not download before kickoff, there is no recovery or second generation.
