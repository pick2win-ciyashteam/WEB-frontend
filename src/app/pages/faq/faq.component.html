FAQ

help Common questions.

/ 01Is my first UCT really free?
Yes. Every new verified account gets one free UCT generation. No coin needed, no card needed. After that, you need a coin pack to continue. Email and mobile must both be verified with a 6-digit OTP before the free match can be claimed.
/ 02Why do you ask for both my mobile number and my email?
PICK2WIN uses dual verification: 6-digit OTPs are sent to both your mobile and your email. One mobile = one email = one account. Dual verification prevents multi-account abuse, helps recover access if you lose one channel, and lets us deliver important notifications reliably.
/ 03Can I create multiple accounts to claim the free match again?
No. PICK2WIN enforces one mobile = one email = one account with device and account checks. Multi-account abuse, fake mobile numbers, or temporary emails used to claim repeated free matches can result in permanent suspension.
/ 04Is there a minimum age to use PICK2WIN?
Yes. PICK2WIN services are strictly intended only for users aged 18 years or older. By creating an account, users confirm they are 18+ and legally permitted to use the platform within their jurisdiction.
/ 05If I delete my account, will I get my unused coins back?
No. If you voluntarily delete your PICK2WIN account, unused coins are permanently forfeited and balances are non-refundable. Before requesting account deletion, fully utilise your purchased coins and complete intended UCT usage.
/ 06How do coin packs work - will I be charged again automatically?
Coin packs are one-time purchases - no subscriptions, no auto-renewal. You buy a pack, coins credit immediately, and you spend them when generating UCTs. Nothing is charged automatically.
/ 07How long are my coins valid for?
All coin packs are valid for 365 days from purchase. If future active-pack merge rules are applied, they will be shown clearly in the pricing and profile coin-lot views. Expired coins cannot be restored.
/ 08Can I buy more than one coin pack and combine them?
Yes. You can purchase any available pack as many times as you like. Coins stack into a single usable balance on your account, while expiry and pack-lot rules are shown in your profile.
/ 09Will I get a refund if a match is cancelled or postponed?
No. Once UCT generation completes successfully, the coin is consumed and the workflow is considered complete. Refunds are not provided for cancelled, postponed, abandoned, rescheduled matches, lineup changes, or third-party fantasy platform actions.
/ 10How are my payments processed?
All payments are processed by Stripe. Every transaction runs through Stripe Checkout with 3D Secure authentication, Radar fraud detection, and card fingerprint deduplication. PICK2WIN never stores card numbers, CVV codes, or banking passwords.
/ 11Who handles payments on PICK2WIN?
Stripe. PICK2WIN partners with Stripe for all coin-pack payments. Stripe handles card processing, 3D Secure authentication, fraud monitoring, receipts, and chargebacks. PICK2WIN does not maintain its own wallet or hold user funds.
/ 12When can I actually run UCT for a match?
UCT activates only after official lineups are released, typically around 60 minutes before kickoff for many major leagues. Before lineup release, the match is locked. Once the official starting XI and substitutes are published, Run UCT becomes active. UCT closes at kickoff.
/ 13Can I run UCT more than once for the same match?
No. Each match supports exactly one successful UCT generation per user. Once UCT generates your 20 teams, the associated coin is consumed, regeneration is blocked, and the match redirects to My Teams.
/ 14How does substitute player selection work?
Substitute selection is optional and user-controlled. You can view the Playing XI and substitute players, then optionally select substitutes to include in the generation pool. If you do not want a substitute considered, leave them unselected.
/ 15What is the difference between Mandate YES and Mandate NO?
Mandate YES means selected players appear in every generated team. Mandate NO means selected players appear in zero generated teams and cannot appear as Captain or Vice-Captain. Both are optional, user-controlled overrides.
/ 16Is choosing the Captain and Vice-Captain mandatory?
Yes. Captain and Vice-Captain configuration is mandatory because captaincy is structurally fundamental to the 20-team workflow. You choose between CVC rotation or structured C and VC pools.
/ 17What is the difference between CVC mode and C and VC mode?
CVC mode lets selected players rotate freely as Captain and Vice-Captain. C and VC mode separates Captain candidates from Vice-Captain candidates for tighter control.
/ 18What happens if 3 or 4 matches release lineups at the same time?
Each match is independent. You can finish one UCT workflow, then start another. Generation completes in seconds, so multiple simultaneous lineup releases are exactly the kind of pre-kickoff pressure PICK2WIN is designed to reduce.
/ 19Why does PICK2WIN always generate exactly 20 teams?
Twenty is the fixed minimum structure for UCT. It supports captain rotation, vice-captain coverage, formation splits, and broader exposure balancing. Smaller counts cannot provide the same structured workflow coverage.
/ 20Can I use PICK2WIN teams directly in my fantasy app?
PICK2WIN produces reference structures, not direct submissions. You take the output to whichever third-party fantasy app you use and adapt it to that app's salary caps, scoring rules, and contest format.
/ 21Do I still need to enter the teams into my fantasy app myself?
Yes. PICK2WIN does not connect to or auto-submit on third-party fantasy apps. After generation, download or copy the teams and submit them to your chosen fantasy app yourself.
/ 22What happens to my teams after the match kicks off?
Generated teams should be saved before kickoff. Download the CSV as soon as generation completes. The CSV is the durable copy you need for fantasy-app entry and later reference.
/ 23How long does PICK2WIN store my generated teams?
Generated teams remain visible for the relevant match window and may later be archived or pruned based on storage policies. The CSV download is the durable copy, so download it immediately after generation.
/ 24So what exactly is PICK2WIN?
PICK2WIN is a structured virtual team configuration and workflow assistance platform designed to help users generate organized team combinations quickly after official lineups are released. Generated outputs are reference structures created from user-selected inputs.
/ 25Does UCT predict match outcomes or guarantee results?
No. UCT does not predict match outcomes, player performance, fantasy points, ranking positions, or contest success. Final participation decisions and contest-entry choices remain fully your responsibility.
/ 26What does PICK2WIN actually take responsibility for?
PICK2WIN's responsibility is limited to football virtual team configuration workflows, structured multi-team generation assistance, and transparent lineup workflow support. We are clear about partners, pricing, account rules, supported markets, and what we do not control.
/ 27Where does PICK2WIN football data come from?
Football data is sourced from a third-party football API provider. Starting XIs, substitute lists, player metadata, fixture timings, and live match status come from that provider. PICK2WIN does not maintain its own lineup database.
/ 28What happens if a lineup is updated after I generated my teams?
Lineup updates can occasionally happen after generation. UCT generation completed using the lineup information available at that time. Later lineup updates do not automatically trigger regeneration, additional runs, or additional coin deductions.
/ 29What if a player shows on PICK2WIN but not in my fantasy app?
Always follow your fantasy app's player list, not PICK2WIN's. If a mismatch appears, adjust your final contest entry to match your fantasy app. If you spot a persistent mismatch, email support@pick2win.io.
/ 30Which countries can use PICK2WIN?
PICK2WIN supports selected markets worldwide. Supported countries may be modified based on operational, compliance, and payment-provider requirements. The signup country list reflects the currently supported countries.
/ 31Which leagues and competitions does PICK2WIN cover?
PICK2WIN covers selected football competitions, including major leagues and tournament fixtures. One coin equals one UCT generation for any covered match. The Lineouts tab shows currently available series and matches.
/ 32Who is PICK2WIN built for, and why does it not use salary caps?
PICK2WIN is built for multi-entry fantasy users who manually create many teams per match. It deliberately does not use salary caps, budgets, or app-specific scoring rules because those mechanics belong to your fantasy app. PICK2WIN focuses on the football-side configuration workflow.
