☑ What PICK2WIN Does

  • Supports multiple sports.
  • Supports multiple fantasy platforms.
  • Supports multiple contest modes.
  • Uses only your selected player pool.
  • Applies contest-specific roster rules and salary-cap validation.

✕ What PICK2WIN Does Not Do

  • Does not recommend players.
  • Does not predict player performance.
  • Does not create teams without your player selections.
  • Does not optimize lineups using projections or AI.
  • Does not submit entries to any fantasy platform.
  • Does not connect to or synchronize with your fantasy account.
  • Does not automatically import or export teams to third-party fantasy platforms.

Using Your Generated Teams

  1. Review your generated lineups.
  2. Download the TXT file.
  3. Open your preferred fantasy platform.
  4. Manually recreate the teams you wish to use using the downloaded TXT file.

PICK2WIN's responsibility ends after generating valid team combinations based on your selections and providing them in a downloadable TXT format.
