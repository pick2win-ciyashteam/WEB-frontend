What is My Squad?+

My Squad is your personal player pool created from official lineups. Every generated lineup uses only the players you selected.

What is a User Configuration Team (UCT)?+

UCT is PICK2WIN's process for organizing and validating your My Squad before generating lineup combinations.

Does PICK2WIN recommend players?+

No. Every player is selected by you, and your strategy always remains under your control.

Does PICK2WIN use AI?+

No. PICK2WIN never recommends players or predicts outcomes.

Can I edit My Squad?+

Yes. You can update your player pool before generating lineup combinations.

How many lineup combinations can I generate?+

You can generate up to 20 rule-valid lineup combinations per successful generation.

Can I review before recreating?+

Yes. Every lineup combination can be reviewed before you recreate it.

Why is PICK2WIN different?+

It helps you prepare efficiently without replacing your knowledge, strategy, or final decisions.
