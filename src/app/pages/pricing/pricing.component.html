365-DAY VALIDITY - ALL PACKS

Every pack is valid for 365 days from purchase.

All four packs - Starter, Basic, Standard, Pro - carry the same 365-day validity, counted from your most recent purchase. Each new pack you buy refreshes the clock on your whole remaining balance. 365 means exactly 365 days - not 12 months, not "1 calendar year", not adjusted for leap years.

sync WHAT IF YOU BUY MORE THAN ONE PACK

Your coins always live as a single combined balance. Buy a new pack while you still have active coins and your remaining coins merge into the new pack - the whole balance is then revalidated for a fresh 365 days from that purchase date. Example: buy a 50-coin pack today; 100 days later you still have coins left and buy a 100-coin pack - your leftover coins merge with the new 100 and the entire balance stays valid for 365 days from that second purchase. You can view your combined balance and its expiry inside My Space - Active Coin Lots.
