📖 1. General Policy

All purchases on PICK2WIN are generally non-refundable.

PICK2WIN operates using:

  • prepaid workflow access
  • coin-based football virtual team generation access

By purchasing coin packs, users acknowledge and agree to this policy.

🪙 2. Coin-Based Usage Policy

PICK2WIN operates using:

  • prepaid digital workflow access
  • structured football virtual team generation workflows
1 Coin = 1 Match UCT Generation

Coins are consumed only when UCT successfully generates football virtual teams for a match.

Once generation completes successfully:

  • the associated coin is permanently consumed
  • the workflow is considered completed

⚙️ 3. Successful UCT Usage

Once UCT generation completes successfully:

  • refunds are not applicable
  • regeneration for the same match is blocked
  • "Run UCT" becomes unavailable for that match

PICK2WIN operational responsibility for that match workflow ends once teams are successfully generated and delivered.

🚫 4. No Refund Situations

Refunds are NOT provided for:

cancelled matches
postponed matches
abandoned matches
interrupted tournaments
lineup changes
reduced football schedules
user dissatisfaction
user mistakes
accidental purchases
unused coins
partially used balances
unsupported user expectations
account deletion requests after purchase
failure to use coins before expiry

🗑️ 5. Account Deletion & Coin Balance Policy

If a user voluntarily deletes their PICK2WIN account:

  • unused coins will NOT be refunded
  • partial balances will NOT be returned
  • coin purchases remain non-refundable
  • deleted accounts cannot reclaim previous balances

Users should fully utilize purchased coins and complete intended UCT usage before requesting account deletion.

Once account deletion is completed:

  • remaining balances are permanently removed
  • account recovery may not be possible

⚖️ 6. Operational Clarification

PICK2WIN operates using prepaid workflow access and structured football virtual team generation services.

Allowing unrestricted refunds after coin purchases, account deletions, or partial usage could negatively impact platform stability, payment operations, fraud-prevention controls, and infrastructure sustainability.

This policy exists to reduce abuse, prevent refund exploitation, and maintain fair usage standards.

🛠️ 7. Technical Failure Exception

Refunds or retries may be considered only if:

  • payment was successful
  • UCT completely failed due to a verified technical issue

Requests are subject to internal verification and operational review.

PICK2WIN reserves the right to approve, reject, retry, or partially resolve technical-failure requests after review.

⏳ 8. Coin Validity

All coin packs are valid for exactly 365 days from the latest successful pack purchase date.

This applies uniformly to all current packs and all future packs introduced under the same coin system.

🔄 Active Pack Merge Rule. If a user purchases a new coin pack before existing coins expire:

  • remaining active coins from previous packs are merged into the newly purchased pack
  • the combined balance receives a fresh 365-day validity period

The refreshed validity period starts from the latest successful pack purchase date.

📌 Validity rules.

  • each new successful pack purchase refreshes validity for all active remaining coins
  • all active balances merge into a unified coin balance
  • expired coins cannot be restored or merged
  • coins already expired before a new purchase remain permanently forfeited
  • remaining coins automatically expire after the active 365-day validity period ends

Users are responsible for monitoring validity periods and planning UCT usage accordingly.

🚫 Expired coins policy. Once coins expire:

  • remaining balances are permanently forfeited
  • expired balances cannot be restored
  • refunds are not provided for expired coins

🚨 9. Chargeback Abuse

Fraudulent chargebacks or payment abuse may result in:

  • permanent account suspension
  • payment blocking
  • restriction of future purchases
  • legal escalation if required

📌 10. Important Platform Disclaimer

PICK2WIN provides:

  • football workflow assistance
  • structured football virtual team generation workflows
  • Captain & Vice-Captain configuration workflows
  • multi-team configuration assistance

PICK2WIN operational responsibility ends once football virtual teams are successfully generated and delivered.

Users independently utilize generated outputs on third-party fantasy football platforms.
