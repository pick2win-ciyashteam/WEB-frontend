1. General Policy

All purchases on PICK2WIN are generally non-refundable.

2. Coin-Based Usage Policy

PICK2WIN operates using prepaid digital workflow access and coin-based football virtual team generation access.

1 Coin = 1 Match UCT Generation.

Coins are consumed only when UCT successfully generates teams for a match.

3. No Refund Situations

Refunds are not provided for:

cancelled matches
postponed matches
abandoned matches
lineup changes
fantasy losses
user dissatisfaction
unused coins
user mistakes
reduced football schedules
tournament interruptions

4. Successful UCT Usage

Once UCT generation completes successfully, the usage is considered consumed and refunds are not applicable.

5. Account Deletion & Coin Balance Policy

If a user voluntarily deletes their PICK2WIN account:
  • unused coins will not be refunded
  • partial balances will not be returned
  • coin purchases remain non-refundable
  • deleted accounts cannot reclaim previous balances

Users should fully utilize purchased coins and complete intended UCT usage before requesting account deletion. Once account deletion is completed, remaining balances are permanently removed and recovery may not be possible.

6. Operational Clarification

Allowing unrestricted refunds after coin purchases, account deletions, or partial usage could negatively impact platform stability, payment operations, fraud prevention systems, and infrastructure sustainability.

This policy exists to reduce abuse, prevent refund exploitation, and maintain fair usage standards.

7. Technical Failure Exception

Refunds or retries may be considered only if payment was successful and UCT completely failed due to a verified technical issue after internal review.

8. Coin Validity

All coin packs are valid for exactly 365 days from the date of purchase.

  • Each pack purchase creates its own 365-day clock.
  • 365 days means exactly 365 calendar days.
  • When users have multiple packs, deductions are taken from the pack with the earliest expiry first.
  • Once a pack's 365-day window closes, remaining coins in that pack are forfeited.
  • Expiry of one pack does not affect any other pack the user purchased.
  • No refunds are issued for coins lost to expiry.

9. Chargeback Abuse

Fraudulent chargebacks may result in permanent account suspension, payment blocking, and legal escalation if required.

10. Important Disclaimer

PICK2WIN provides football workflow assistance and structured football virtual team generation. It does not provide betting services, gambling services, wallet balances, withdrawals, or guaranteed winnings.

Users independently utilize generated outputs on third-party fantasy football platforms.
