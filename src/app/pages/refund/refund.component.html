Refund Policy

Last updated 8 June 2026 · PICK2WIN Technologies Pvt Ltd

→ 1One-time purchases

Coin packs are one-time purchases. There are no subscriptions and nothing is ever charged automatically — you choose when to buy another pack.

→ 2Coin validity & Active Pack Merge

Your coins live as a single combined balance valid for 365 days from your most recent purchase. Buying a new pack while you still hold active coins merges your remaining coins into the new pack and revalidates the whole balance for a fresh 365 days from that purchase date. Coins already expired before a new purchase stay forfeited.

→ 3Completed generations are non-refundable

Once a UCT generation completes successfully, the coin is consumed and the workflow is complete. Consumed coins and completed generations are non-refundable.

→ 4Match changes & third-party actions

Refunds are not provided for cancelled, postponed, abandoned or rescheduled matches, lineup changes, reduced fixture schedules, or the actions of any third-party fantasy platform. PICK2WIN’s responsibility ends at delivery of your 20 teams.

→ 5Account deletion forfeits unused coins

If you voluntarily delete your account, unused coins are permanently forfeited and balances are non-refundable. Use your coins before requesting deletion. Once processed, balances are removed and recovery is not possible.

→ 6Verified technical failure

The only exception: if your payment succeeded but a UCT completely failed to generate due to a technical issue on our side, contact support — we will restore the coin or issue a refund after verification.

→ 7Chargebacks

Please contact us before raising a chargeback so we can resolve the issue. Chargebacks raised in bad faith on consumed coins may lead to account suspension.

→ 8Contact

Refund questions? Email support@pick2win.io — we reply within 48 hours.
