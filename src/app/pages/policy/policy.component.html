1. Information Collected

PICK2WIN may collect full name, email address, mobile number, country, IP address, browser / device information, payment references, and usage history.

Payments are securely processed by Stripe.

PICK2WIN does not store full card details, CVV numbers, or banking passwords.

2. Why Information Is Used

  • Account verification.
  • OTP validation.
  • Payment processing.
  • Fraud prevention.
  • Customer support.
  • Platform security.
  • Service improvement.

3. Data Security

PICK2WIN uses encrypted HTTPS connections, OTP verification, secure infrastructure, fraud monitoring, and Stripe PCI-compliant payment systems.

4. Third-Party Services

PICK2WIN may use trusted providers for payments, hosting, OTP delivery, security monitoring, and analytics.

5. Data Sharing

PICK2WIN does not sell user data.

Data may be shared only with Stripe, infrastructure providers, OTP / email providers, and fraud / security systems for operational purposes only.

6. User Rights

Users may request account deletion, data correction, or support assistance through support@pick2win.io.

7. Account Deletion Policy

If a user voluntarily deletes their PICK2WIN account:
  • unused coins will not be refunded
  • partial balances will not be returned
  • deleted accounts cannot reclaim previous balances
  • remaining coin balances are permanently removed

Users are strongly advised to fully utilize purchased coins and complete intended UCT usage before requesting account deletion.

8. Age Restriction

PICK2WIN services are strictly for users aged 18 years or older.
