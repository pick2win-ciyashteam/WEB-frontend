📖 1. Information Collected

PICK2WIN may collect:

  • 👤 full name
  • 📧 email address
  • 📱 mobile number
  • 🌍 country
  • 🌐 IP address
  • 💳 payment references
  • 📊 usage history

Payments are securely processed by Stripe.

🔒 2. Payment & Data Security

PICK2WIN does NOT store:

  • full card details
  • CVV numbers
  • banking passwords

PICK2WIN uses:

  • encrypted HTTPS connections
  • OTP verification
  • secure infrastructure

to help protect user information.

⚙️ 3. Why Information Is Used

Information may be used for:

  • account verification
  • OTP validation
  • payment processing
  • fraud prevention
  • customer support
  • platform security
  • operational support

🔗 4. Third-Party Services

PICK2WIN may use trusted third-party providers for:

  • payment processing
  • hosting infrastructure
  • OTP delivery
  • email delivery

These providers may process limited operational data required for service functionality.

📤 5. Data Sharing

PICK2WIN does NOT sell user data.

Information may be shared only with:

  • Stripe
  • infrastructure providers
  • OTP and email providers

for operational purposes only.

👤 6. User Rights

Users may request:

  • account deletion
  • data correction
  • support assistance

through 📧 support@pick2win.io.

🗑️ 7. Account Deletion Policy

If a user voluntarily deletes their PICK2WIN account:

  • unused coins will NOT be refunded
  • deleted balances cannot be restored
  • remaining balances are permanently removed

Users are advised to fully utilize purchased coins and complete intended UCT usage before requesting account deletion.

🔞 8. Age Restriction

PICK2WIN services are strictly intended only for users aged 18 years or older.
