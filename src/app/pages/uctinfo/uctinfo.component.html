tune The Engine Behind PICK2WIN

A structured football virtual team configuration engine.

UCT helps users create multiple balanced team combinations quickly after official lineups are released. It reduces repetitive manual configuration effort while keeping every decision under user control. Once lineups are available, UCT transforms user-selected configurations into 20 structured football virtual teams within a few seconds.

rocket_launch How To Run UCT

Five simple steps from lineup release to 20 teams.

Using UCT follows the same structured flow every time. Each status is clearly shown inside the Lineouts page.

Step 1

format_list_bulleted Open Lineouts

View supported matches, competition details, kickoff times, lineup status, UCT availability, and your coin balance.

Step 2

schedule Wait for lineups

Before official lineups release, matches remain visible with UCT locked. Generation is unavailable until confirmed team news.

Step 3

verified Lineup released

Once official lineups are available, the Run UCT action opens. Review the playing XI and substitutes before configuration.

Step 4

paid Check coins

1 coin = 1 match UCT generation. Coins are consumed only after successful generation.

Step 5

play_arrow Run UCT

Choose the match, start the four-screen workflow, confirm responsibility, and generate your structured teams.

schema UCT Configuration Workflow

Four configuration screens, one confirmation, 20 teams.

All selections are made entirely by the user. PICK2WIN does not recommend players, alter configurations, or modify selections. UCT simply processes your inputs and produces structured combinations.

Screen 1 Optional

person_add Substitute Selection

Users may select up to 3 substitute players, or skip substitute selection entirely.

  • M-YES: selected substitute appears in all generated teams
  • NA: no forced inclusion
  • M-NO is not allowed for substitutes
Screen 2 Optional

rule Mandate Selection

Mandates allow users to control player inclusion across all 20 teams.

  • M-YES: 1-2 players appear in every generated team
  • M-NO: 1-2 players appear in zero generated teams
  • NA: no inclusion or exclusion preference
Screen 3 Required

workspace_premium Captain & Vice-Captain

This step is mandatory. Without it, team generation cannot continue.

  • CVC mode: 2-4 players rotate as both Captain and Vice-Captain
  • C&VC mode: separate Captain and Vice-Captain pools
  • M-NO players cannot be Captain or Vice-Captain
Screen 4 Review

fact_check Configuration Summary

Review substitute, mandate, and captaincy selections before generation.

  • Review configuration
  • Go back and modify selections
  • Create teams after confirmation

sports_soccer Team Composition Spec

Each structured team is an 11-player squad drawn from both real teams.

Every generated team follows football-real composition rules. The engine never invents players and never recommends picks.

Squad size 11 players

Total per team, the standard football starting XI.

Team source Both teams

Players are selected from both real clubs playing the match.
