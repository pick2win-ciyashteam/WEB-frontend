02 - ACCESS - 5 STEPS

How to Run UCT.

Five simple steps from lineup release to 20 teams.

Using UCT follows the same structured flow every time. Each status is clearly shown inside the Lineouts page.

Open Lineouts

Step 2

05 - GOALKEEPER STRATEGY

Goalkeeper Strategy.

Goalkeepers work differently from outfield players in UCT.

1 GKEvery team needs one

Each of the 20 generated teams must include exactly one goalkeeper.

2Starting goalkeepers

A standard match gives UCT two starting goalkeepers from the confirmed lineups.

DEF/MID/FWDMandates only

Mandates apply only to DEF, MID, FWD. Goalkeepers follow coverage rules.

sports_soccer
UCT splits both goalkeepers across the 20 teams.

By default, UCT distributes the 2 starting goalkeepers across the 20 generated teams in a balanced split. Both GKs appear - no manual configuration needed before generation.

add_circle
If you pick a substitute goalkeeper

Substitute selection can include a goalkeeper. If you add a third goalkeeper as a substitute in Screen 1, UCT distributes all 3 goalkeepers across the 20 teams.

Your goalkeeper coverage is set by what you pick in the Substitute step, before you generate. Once UCT generates, the 20 teams are final.

09 - CONSUMPTION RULE

One Match = One UCT.

Each match supports one successful UCT generation per user.

lock

Once generation completes, 1 coin is consumed, regeneration is blocked, and the workflow is considered completed for that match. This rule keeps coin economics simple and prevents accidental double-spending.

MUST READ

Before you run UCT - read these 6 points.

Each point below is critical to how UCT works for your match. Read them once. After that, you'll know exactly what to expect every time you Run UCT.

01

Generated teams are final.

Once UCT generates your 20 teams, they are locked as-is. PICK2WIN does not allow edits, swaps, or in-place modifications on the generated teams. While teams are visible in My Teams before kickoff, the only actions are browse / preview and download CSV.

02

Download before kickoff.

Please download your 20 teams before the match kicks off. After kickoff, the teams are removed from My Teams - the downloaded CSV becomes your only copy. Use it as per your wish.

03

Coin consumption rule.

UCT consumes 1 coin per match only when generation succeeds and 20 teams land in My Teams. No coin is deducted if the run fails, you cancel mid-flow, or generation never completes.

04

UCT runs are sequential, not parallel.

If two or more matches kick off close together and you want UCT for each, the runs are processed one after another, not simultaneously. Each match keeps its own 20 teams independently.

05

Coin balance is checked before Run UCT.

Before you can click Run UCT, your coin balance is verified. If your balance is 0 coins, the action redirects you to purchase a coin pack.

06

If UCT run fails.

If UCT generation fails for any reason, you're returned to the Substitute Selection screen with your inputs preserved. You can edit your config or keep it the same and re-run UCT. No coin is deducted on failure.

Exception - coin deducted but no teams generated

In the rare case a coin is deducted but no teams appear in My Teams, email us. We'll verify and help ASAP.
