How the platform structures goalkeepers across the 20 teams - these are platform limits and final-team-structure facts, not recommendations.

compare_arrows
UCT splits both goalkeepers across the 20 teams.

By default, UCT distributes the 2 starting goalkeepers across the 20 generated teams in a balanced split. Both GKs appear - no manual configuration needed before generation.

Example: GK from Team A appears in some teams - GK from Team B appears in the others - both covered across the full 20.

add_circle
If you pick a substitute goalkeeper

Substitute selection can include a goalkeeper. If you choose to add a third goalkeeper as a substitute in the Substitute step, UCT distributes all 3 goalkeepers across the 20 teams.

workspace_premium
Goalkeepers in captaincy - how the structure works

Each generated team contains exactly one goalkeeper. A single team can never hold both a Captain goalkeeper and a Vice-Captain goalkeeper, and two different goalkeepers can never be the Captain and Vice-Captain of the same team. In captaincy configuration, a goalkeeper resolves to a single role - Captain or Vice-Captain - not a paired CVC rotation. This is a property of the platform's 20-team structure, not a recommendation.

All decisions are entirely yours. PICK2WIN never recommends one option over another.

Two captaincy modes to choose how Captain and Vice-Captain are distributed across the 20 generated teams. Pick whichever fits your strategy.

sync

CVC Mode

Pick 2-4 players. They rotate freely as both Captain and Vice-Captain across the 20 teams. This is a single combined pool: every selected player can appear in either role according to the generated team structure.

  • One shared C/VC pool
  • Broader captaincy rotation
  • Simple balanced coverage

MUST READ - 6 POINTS

Before you run UCT - read these 6 points.

Each point below is critical to how UCT works for your match. Read them once - after that, you'll know exactly what to expect every time you Run UCT.

01

Generated teams are final.

Once UCT generates your 20 teams, they are locked as-is. PICK2WIN does not allow edits, swaps, or in-place modifications on the generated teams. While teams are visible in My Teams before kickoff, the only actions are browse / preview and download TXT.

02

Download before kickoff.

Please download your 20 teams before the match kicks off. After kickoff, the teams are removed from My Teams - the downloaded TXT becomes your only copy. Use it as per your wish.

03

Coin consumption rule.

UCT consumes 1 coin per match only when generation succeeds and 20 teams land in My Teams. No coin is deducted if the run fails, you cancel mid-flow, or generation never completes.

04

UCT runs are sequential, not parallel.

If two or more matches kick off close together and you want UCT for each, the runs are processed one after another, not simultaneously. Each match keeps its own 20 teams independently.

05

Coin balance is checked before Run UCT.

Before you can click Run UCT, your coin balance is verified. If your balance is 0 coins, the action redirects you to purchase a coin pack.

06

If UCT run fails.

If UCT generation fails for any reason, you're returned to the Substitute Selection screen with your inputs preserved. You can edit your config or keep it the same and re-run UCT. No coin is deducted on failure.

Exception - coin deducted but no teams generated

In the rare case a coin is deducted but no teams appear in My Teams, email us. We'll verify and help ASAP.
