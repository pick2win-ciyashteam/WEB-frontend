What is PICK2WIN UCT?

PICK2WIN UCT (User Configuration Teams) is a virtual DFS lineup builder designed exclusively for daily real contests. It works only after the official starting lineups (Playing XI) for a match are announced.

Not a weekly fantasy manager Not a season-long game Not a prediction engine Not a match simulator

Instead, it helps you prepare multiple strategic team combinations for an individual contest — based entirely on your own sports knowledge and decisions.

How UCT Works

1
Choose a Match. Select your preferred fantasy platform and choose any supported contest after the official starting lineups are announced.
2
Build Your Squad. Select the players you want to include in My Squad from the official starting lineups — only the players you select will be considered for team generation.
3
Configure Your Strategy. Complete your configuration based on the selected fantasy platform. Options and their sequence may vary between platforms — you may configure My Squad, Mandatory Players (M-YES), Captain Pool, Captain / Vice-Captain selection, Salary Cap, team-combination rules and other platform-specific options. PICK2WIN UCT automatically displays only the options applicable to your selected platform.
4
Review Your Configuration. Carefully review your complete configuration before generating — verify your selected match, My Squad, Mandatory Players, Captain / Vice-Captain selections, team-combination rules and platform-specific settings. Once you generate teams, 1 coin is deducted from your account for that match.
5
Generate Teams. Click Generate Teams — PICK2WIN UCT creates up to 20 strategic team combinations within seconds, based entirely on your selected players and configuration.
6
Review & Submit. Review every generated team and choose the lineups you want to submit on your preferred fantasy platform. The final submission and contest entry always remain under your control.

👥 My Squad rules

  • Build from the official Starting XI + eligible substitutes of both teams.
  • Squad size: 18–22 players; any number in each position.
  • There must be at least 1 goalkeeper in your My Squad.
  • 0–3 substitutes from DEF / MID / FWD only.
  • Enter the DraftKings salary (cap value) for every selected player.
  • Players not in My Squad are never used.

⭐ Mandatory player (M-YES)

  • Completely optional — select 0 or 1 player.
  • Your Mandatory player appears in all 20 lineups.
  • Positions: DEF / MID / FWD only — no goalkeepers.
  • Why no GK mandate? Every lineup already needs one GK, so a mandate on an outfield player adds more value.

💰 Salary cap priority

  • Selecting a player doesn’t guarantee they appear in every lineup.
  • The engine builds strictly within the $50,000 salary cap.
  • A player that breaks the cap may be excluded from a lineup.
  • Every generated lineup is salary-cap compliant and valid.

🧩 DraftKings position structure (8)

  • 8 players: 1 GK + 2 DEF + 2 MID + 2 FWD + 1 UTIL.
  • UTIL is a 3rd outfielder (DEF / MID / FWD), never a goalkeeper — so every lineup is one of three formations: (1,3,2,2), (1,2,3,2) or (1,2,2,3).
  • The GK is always a starting keeper (the substitute-GK rule keeps it in the XI).
  • Team split 1–7 per club — max 7 from one club, so at least one player comes from the opposing club.
  • Salary cap: $50,000 total.

📝 Important notes

  • Lineups built from DraftKings rules + your My Squad + Mandatory (if any) + the $50,000 cap.
  • All 20 lineups are unique — no duplicates.
  • Delivered in milliseconds to seconds.
  • Review in My Teams before entering DraftKings contests.

🎯 Key takeaways

  • Build My Squad (18–22) and enter a DraftKings salary for every player.
  • One optional Mandatory player (DEF / MID / FWD).
  • Captain & Vice-Captain are not used.
  • Up to 20 salary-cap compliant lineups per 1 coin.

📌 · MUST READ · 6 POINTS

Before you run UCT — read these 6 points.

Each point below is critical to how UCT works for your match. Read them once — after that, you’ll know exactly what to expect every time you Run UCT.

01

Generated teams are final.

Once UCT generates your 20 teams, they are locked as-is. PICK2WIN does not allow edits, swaps, or in-place modifications on the generated teams. While teams are visible in My Teams before kickoff, the only actions are browse / preview and download TXT.

02

Download before kickoff.

Please download your 20 teams before the match kicks off. After kickoff, the teams are removed from My Teams — the downloaded TXT becomes your only copy. Use it as per your wish.

03

Coin consumption policy.

UCT consumes 1 coin per successful UCT — each successful run of up to 20 teams saved to My Teams. 1 coin = 1 successful UCT. Because every fantasy platform generates an independent set of teams, running UCT for both apps (DraftKings, FanDuel) on the same match consumes 2 coins — 1 per successful UCT. No coin is deducted if generation fails, you cancel before it starts, the run doesn’t complete, or the teams aren’t saved to My Teams. Coins stay valid for 365 days across any supported match, league or platform.

04

UCT runs are sequential, not parallel.

If two or more matches kick off close together and you want UCT for each, the runs are processed one after another, not simultaneously. Each match keeps its own 20 teams independently.

05

Coin balance is checked before Run UCT.

Before you can click Run UCT, your coin balance is verified. If your balance is 0 coins, the action redirects you to purchase a coin pack.

06

If UCT run fails.

If UCT generation fails for any reason, you’re returned to the Substitute Selection screen with your inputs preserved. You can edit your config or keep it the same and re-run UCT. No coin is deducted on failure.
