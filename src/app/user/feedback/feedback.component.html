PICK2WIN has many version updates planned. But the best refinements come from you.

Your votes, comments, and bug reports tell us what genuinely saves users time and brings real satisfaction to people using UCT every gameweek.

What can you tell us?

Six kinds of feedback - all read by a real person within 48 hours.

target

UCT tuning requests

"Increase variance on captains" - "Add 8-3 / 3-8 splits" - "Hard-lock a specific formation" - anything about how the engine generates teams.

settings

Feature suggestions

New exports, integrations, workflow shortcuts, presets, team-comparison views - anything that would make your workflow smoother.

emoji_events

League / coverage requests

Want a league we do not cover yet? A specific cup competition? Women's football? Tell us which competition matters most to you.

science

Engine accuracy feedback

Noticed a player missing? Lineup did not refresh in time? Constraint validation behaved oddly? The engineering team triages these within 24 hours.

bug_report

Bug reports

UI glitches, mobile issues, payment problems, account access - anything not working as expected. Include screenshots if you can.

favorite

What you love

Equally important - what is working well? Knowing what users genuinely value helps us protect those things during future changes.

Our commitment to feedback senders

  • Every submission is read within 48 hours by a real person on the PICK2WIN team - no auto-replies, no triage shortcuts.
  • Top-voted requests reviewed weekly in our product call every Tuesday. Decisions and timelines are emailed back when possible.
  • Status updates go directly to your inbox - Acknowledged, In Review, Planned, Declined, or Shipped.
  • No suggestion is too small. Typo fixes, copy improvements, and layout bugs all get product triage.
